Our Token
What is Dryad Token?

Dryad is a token, based on the Binance Smart Chain technology. It is the core asset of the Dryad Projects.
01

A 100% project token

The objective of the token is to finance future projects betting on green technologies and create research projects to improve them.
02

Protects the identity

The token is based on blockchain technology so absolutely no investor data is recorded. Our idea is to grow the token to create a sustainable economy over time that benefits all parties.
03

No transaction fees

We have no need to charge fees because it is not in the spirit of the venture. The basic idea grow the value of the token so that it can exist on its own in the free market.
04

Transfers without intermediaries

We are not a bank nor are we looking to behave like one. The transfers are P2P the tokens are yours and you decide what to do with them.
